About

Kung Fu Slipper was formed by a group of musicians with a shared love of improvised music.

The majority of us have played in various musical projects together over the years and "The Slipper" provides the perfect outlet for exploration of a wide variety of musical ideas.

Rhythm plays a big part in the way the band works and because of this the music stays accessible whilst taking the listener on a musical journey visiting many different genres including Jazz, Funk, Blues, Afrobeat, Reggae and much more…

The improvisational elements sometimes use an existing idea as a "head" which allows for the band to improvise around that starting point—following a standard approach used by many Jazz bands. Other times, a musical idea will happen on the spot and the band will listen to and spark off each other, to expand on that idea and see where it goes.

Join us on the journey…
